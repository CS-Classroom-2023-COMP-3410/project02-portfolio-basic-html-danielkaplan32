Stable Roommates

Developed as an independent project, StableRoommates is a lightweight application that implements Irving's Stable Roommates algorithm to generate stable pairings from ranked preferences.

Built to guarantee that no two individuals would both prefer to be paired with each other over their assigned matches, StableRoommates avoids unstable outcomes in group matching scenarios.

Users can enter names and preferences manually or invite others to submit their rankings through unique links, with organizers able to monitor submissions and share results easily.

The tool is great for use in classrooms, study groups, and events where structured, stable pairings are needed.


StudyPilot

Developed as an independent/work study project, StudyPilot uses an LLM to instantly convert notes into quizzes, flashcards, and study guides.

Designed to save time and improve retention with spaced repetition, StudyPilot streamlines the creation of study resources that are often timely and tedious to make by hand.

Users can quickly generate personalized materials, test themselves, and manually adjust content for their unique learning needs.

Additional features include progress tracking, daily challenges, and accessibility options to support diverse study styles.
